It's Never Too Late To Start Living Well

Well Being

Wellbeing is a term that describes how well you feel about yourself and your life in general. Wellbeing is not just a feeling or emotion – nor is it the absence of disease. Instead, it’s the combination of feeling and being physically, mentally and emotionally healthy. Experiencing wellbeing is linked to overall health, happiness and contentment in life.

Yoga

Yoga is suitable for everyone, no matter what age, gender or level of fitness, there is something for everyone.

Meditation

Meditation can improve well-being and quality of life. Recent studies have offered promising results about the impact of meditation in reducing blood pressure.

The Science of Wellbeing

"The Science of Well-Being” taught by Professor Laurie Santos overviews what psychological science says about happiness.
